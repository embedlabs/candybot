Detailed Description

The RSSFeedObserver receives notifications and callbacks indicating the success or failure of attempts to read an RSSFeed.

Every call to RSSFeed.requestNextItem() will result in two callbacks:

  • feedDidRequestNextItem(RSSFeed), (when the item is requested)

and one of either:

  • feedDidReceiveNextItem(RSSFeed, RSSItem),
  • feedDidFailToReceiveNextItem(RSSFeed, Exception),

depending on whether the request succeeded or failed.

See also:
RSSFeed, RSSItem
