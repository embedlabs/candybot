Detailed Description

A Policy determines which RSSItems will be loaded from the list of that is returned from the server. A Policy specifies the filtering criteria on which items loaded from the server will be returned. Scoreloop provides some pre-defined policies:

  • STANDARD_POLICY ,
  • STICKY_POLICY ,
  • UNREAD_POLICY , and
  • ON_START_POLICY .

You are also free to define your own policies, if desired.
