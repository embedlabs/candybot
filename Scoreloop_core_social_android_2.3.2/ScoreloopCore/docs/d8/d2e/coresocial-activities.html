Activities

Q. What do activities refer to?
Developers are free to stream user updates directly to their game. These updates are referred to as "activities". Two lists of activities may be requested from Scoreloop:

  • the activities of the session user's buddies, (across all games),
  • the activities of all the users playing the current game,
  • community related activities (activities across all games and users)
  • the activities of a user(across all games),
  • the activities of all users playing a particular game.

Q. What Scoreloop events count as "activities"?
Activities will relate to one of the following Scoreloop events:

  • a Score,
  • a Challenge,
  • an Achievement.Â 

Q. What might an activity message look like?
An example message, (relating to a score-based Scoreloop event), might read something like: "Player001 has scored 50 points in the game SLDemoCore".
