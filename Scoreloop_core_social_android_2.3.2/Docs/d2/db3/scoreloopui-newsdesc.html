News

Q. What is the 'news' feature?
The news feature lets you communicate directly with players of your game by displaying news items within the game.

Q. Where do these items get displayed?
In the 'News' view of the ScoreloopUI interface. This is directly accessible from the Scoreloop Entry Screen.

Q. How do I decide what items get displayed?
You need to configure which items you would like to be displayed by visiting https://developer.scoreloop.com and navigating to the 'Feed' page. You could either choose to have:

  • news items explicitly specified by you on the Scoreloop admin site - e.g. to push promotional information to the game users, or
  • activities of other users of the game automatically generated by the Scoreloop server, or
  • both of the above.

Q. What type of configuration options do I have?
On https://developer.scoreloop.com you can define certain items as being "sticky" which means that they will appear at the top of the news screen even after they have been read. You may also choose the type of news items that will be displayed: just the news feed defined by you, or else a combination of the news feed and Scoreloop-related activities).

Q. Can I change what items get displayed?
Yes. Simply go to https://developer.scoreloop.com and edit/delete your configured news items.
