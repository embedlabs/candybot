Buddies

Q. What are 'buddy users'?
A user may designate other Scoreloop users as friends and add them to a 'buddy-list'. Adding someone to your buddy-list is unidirectional, (you will not be automatically added to their buddy-list, for example).

Q. What is my 'buddy-list'?
ScoreloopUI lets you designate other users as 'buddies' and add them to a list that is associated with your Scoreloop user profile. A user's buddy-list can be used to generate a leaderboard composed of the best scores submitted by their friends. You should see the guide to the Buddies View for details about how to add to the buddy-list of the session user.

Q. What is the buddy-list used for?
The buddy-list is used in the following scenarios:

  • to directly challenge a buddy,
  • to filter scores, (i.e. to generate the 'buddy-list' leaderboard).

Q. How do I add people to my buddy-list?
You can add other users to your buddy-list from the ScoreloopUI Buddy View. You should make sure, however, if you wish to allow users to add buddies from their local address book that the ADDRESS_BOOK permission is set in the Android Manifest

Q. Can I remove people from my buddy-list?
Yes. Launching the ScoreloopUI Buddies View from within your game will let users remove buddies from their list.

Q. Can I only add Scoreloop users to the list?
Yes. Only existing Scoreloop users can be added to your list.
