Social Market

Q. What is the social market?
The social market is the way we promote Scoreloop-enabled games to other users.

Q. How does it work?
Launching the Social Market view displays links to:

  • New games,
  • Featured games,
  • Most popular games,
  • Games played by the user's friends,
  • Games played by the user.

Q. Can users download games directly from the Social Market view?
Yes. Clicking on a game link from the market view lets users download and install games directly onto their devices.

Q. What games appear in the market view??
The "most popular" list is generated server-side based on live data. The "friends' game' list in the market view is based on the games played by users from your buddy-list.

Q. What does this mean for my game?
The Social Market offers new promotional opportunities for your game. Users will see if their friends are playing your game and can download it directly.

Q. How do I implement the market?
The market is directly available from the shortcuts panel on every ScoreloopUI screen. See the integration guide for the Social Market view for more details.
