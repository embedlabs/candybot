Social Messaging

Q. What is social messaging?
Social messaging is the sending of messages from within the game to:

  • supported social networks,
  • email.

Q. What social networks are currently supported?
The CoreSocial SDK currently supports posting to:

  • Facebook
  • Twitter

Q. What Scoreloop events can I send messages about?
Currently CoreSocial lets users post messages when they:

  • achieve a score,
  • create a challenge,
  • win a challenge,
  • achieve an award,
  • want to promote a game, or
  • want to invite someone to join Scoreloop.

Q. What classes are involved in social messaging?
Social networks are modelled using the SocialProvider class. The following controllers are also involved:

  • SocialProviderController, (to connect to a social network),,
  • MessageController, (sends messages to social networks or email).
