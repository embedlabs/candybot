News

Q. What is the 'news' feature?
The news feature lets you communicate directly with players of your game by displaying news items within the game.

Q. How do I decide what items get displayed?
You need to configure which items you would like to be displayed by visiting https://developer.scoreloop.com and navigating to the 'Feed' page. You can choose to display:

  1. news items explicitly specified by you on the Scoreloop developer site - e.g. to push promotional information to the game users, or
  2. automatically generated user activity items,
  3. both of the above.

Q. Can I change what items get displayed?
Yes. Simply go to https://developer.scoreloop.com and edit/delete your configured news items.

Q. What type of configuration options do I have?
On https://developer.scoreloop.com you can define certain items as being "sticky" which means that they will appear at the top of the news screen even after they have been read.
