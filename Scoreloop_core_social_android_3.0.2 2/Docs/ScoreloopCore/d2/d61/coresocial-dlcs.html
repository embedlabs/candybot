Game Items (Virtual Goods)

Q. What are Game Items?
Game Items are virtual goods defined by game developers and distributed through Scoreloop ºs Infrastructure. They can carry binary data as a form of downloadable content. Game developers can offer Game Items to users for free, purchasable through In App purchases for money or the in-game currency.

Q. What are the typical Game Items?
Game-items could be:

  • additional coins for playing challenges
  • additional levels
  • additional game avatars or skins
  • additional characters or
  • any content, that can make your game more valueable and what players might desire to buy separately.

Q. What are Coin Packs?
Coin packs are special type of game-items, which can be purchased multiple times while the "regular" game items can be purchased or owned just once.

Q. How and where should I configure the Game Items?
Please go to the Game Items section on https://developer.scoreloop.com and create the Game Items there. You will have to specify some parameters such as name, description, price tier etc of the Game Items. You can also upload additional content that can be downloaded when the user owns the item.

Q. What are Price Tiers?
Price Tiers are to configure the real world price of the packs, or the amount of in-game currency they represent. Please note that we follow the world trend, and don't let you put any price but we just let you choose from some predefined price tiers.

Q. Can Image Icons be added for Game Items?
Image Icons may be attached to the Game Items. You could upload it via the Game Items section on https://developer.scoreloop.com .

Q. Can you add additional data for Game Items?
You could associate meta-data as additional data to your game Items. You may use this for any house-keeping activities etc.

Q. How are the Game Items transmitted?
Game Items are transmitted to the client device via HTTP protocol. If you need a more secure variant you need to encrypt the uploaded data and add a decrypt routine into your app.

Q. What are the supported methods of payments to get the Game Items ?
The following options are available:

  • Google Market In App Billing
  • Google Checkout
  • Credit Card (through Wirecard)
  • PayPal
  • In-Game Currency

  • Scoreloop CoreSocial SDK for Android
